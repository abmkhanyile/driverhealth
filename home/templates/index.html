Driver Training

Learn More About Our Truck Driver Training Plan

E-Learning and Truck Inspection

E-learning develops the theoretical base knowledge of the trainee and teaches the trainee the theory, vehicle basics and terminology. Vehicle Inspection teaches the trainee how to perform pre-trip and post-trip vehicle inspections and how to report the findings.

Learn More

Advanced Truck Simulator Training

Our Simulator Training addresses basic to advanced topics including reversing, parking, docking, risky situations, crash avoidance with other vehicles, pedestrians, and animals, as well as driving in adverse weather, at night, hauling oversize or overweight loads, and much more.

Learn More

In-Truck Superlink Training

This is our comprehensive and in-depth practical training on driving a superlink truck. Our trainees are taught how to reverse, park, alley dock, and drive a superlink truck. Trainees will also be taught about Load Securing, Route Planning, Green Band Driving, Defensive Driving and much more.
